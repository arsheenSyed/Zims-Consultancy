  Where we are

About US

ZIMS is a Home tuition consultancy servicing students' communities from different streams. We provide expert mentors to address the issues of different students at different levels.
This organisation grew from a tiny seed into a large tree to provide fruits, shade, and oxygen to learners from all walks of life in India and abroad. We gave shape to a concept that did not exist in India, and we have been involved in education and fields related to it for ten years.
We provide mentors for All subjects till 10,11,12 for ICSE, CBSE, IB, Language Class, MBBS, BDS, Commerce, ICWA and CA tuitions, Students can visit our academy. If they seek any tutor we can provide a tutor at their residence.
Since more than fifteen years ago, ZIMS (ZEAL TO INTEGRATE MERITORIOUS STUDENTS) has provided professional education and tutoring services to children and teenagers from Asia, America, and Africa who have a range of educational challenges. We interact with kids of diverse ages and socioeconomic backgrounds on a wide range of needs and levels.
We provide for the needs of students pursuing MBBS, IIT, EAMCET, B.Ed, and Polytechnics, communication skills, as well as those with more specialised requirements who need an alternative learning and support programme. Since every child is unique, they all need unique care and instruction. Going "back to basics" and laying a strong foundation for certain kids requires a multi-sensory approach that incorporates sight, hearing, touch, creative thinking, and always with unending tolerance, compassion, and humour.
Whether it is dyslexia, dyspepsia, ADHD, high levels of intelligence, or school or college dropouts. We impart to pupils the methods and skills that support and foster self-assurance, a healthy interest in learning, and a love of education.
My reputation and the outcomes of the previous years have helped us build a career. I firmly feel that these elements combine to create a quiet, secure environment that is comfortable, nurturing, and caring. I work to instil confidence by understanding each person's needs and capabilities without passing judgement.
A fragile child can only be inspired to succeed and accomplish through trust. Teaching and sharing my knowledge and enthusiasm for learning have always been my passions. We enjoy our work and still feel the same thrill and fulfilment when we make a discovery or advance, which we also share with the kids. Please explore our website and get in touch with us whenever you have questions about your child's specific needs.
